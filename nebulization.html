About Nubulization Procedure

Nebulization is a procedure where medication is delivered as a mist to help make breathing easier by opening up the airways and keeping them patent (open). This process helps to manage respiratory conditions, ensuring the airways remain clear and reducing symptoms like wheezing, shortness of breath, or coughing.
